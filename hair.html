Barb has 31 years of experience as a professional hair stylist. She has a passion for all her clients; especially the elderly. Barb enjoys creating the latest trendy styles and colors for her clients. She specializes in perms, color, hi-lights and low-lights. Developing strong relationships is the foundation of everything Barb does both professionally and personally. Her clients are her #1 asset and she values their patronage. Barb feels very thankful and blessed in her profession!

Ashley is a cosmetologist with more than 10 years’ experience. She has a passion for all things hair! She specializes in all different types of color such as balayage, ombré, root smudge, traditional foiling, and dimensional color. Ashley keeps up to date with all the current trends on haircuts and styles to truly enhance your own beauty and create the perfect balance of movement and flare. Your hair integrity is her top priority, and she will work hard to maintain the health of your hair and give you the look you want, every time! She loves all of her clients who quickly become more like family!

Amy has been coloring, foiling, cutting, perming, conditioning & styling hair in the beauty industry for more than 22 years. She loves helping people feel beautiful and more confident as they leave the salon. She is blessed to have a career that she is passionate about. Her clients are not only customers but lifelong friends.

Angie has a true love for her trade and feels blessed every day with a career in an industry that provides so many opportunities. She has been a beautician for 28 years and specializes in cuts and color services for your entire family; focusing on gray coverage and enhancing your natural beauty. When you sit in her chair, you have her undivided attention. As a beautician, Angie’s goal is to keep your hair as healthy as possible. As an artist, she loves helping you express yourself and believes that when you look beautiful you feel beautiful. Her favorite thing is helping you feel like the “Masterpiece” you already are!

Kate is a professional hair colorist doing what she truly loves every day. She takes pride in building relationships with her clients and giving them confidence. Kate graduated from Huntington School of Beauty Culture and has been a part of the industry since early 2016. She specializes in blonding, balayage, color melting, traditional highlighting, babylights, dimensional blondes, and all types of hair coloring. She also offers Olaplex treatments and is an advocate for all healthy hair care methods. The integrity of your hair is Kates #1 priority! The most rewarding part of her career is the moment her client sees the final result and the smile on their face after the transformation. Kate hopes to make your experience therapeutic for mind, body, and soul. You can find her on Facebook and Instagram (@stylistkate_).

Michelle has been doing hair for the past 25 years. She enjoys all types of cuts which include men, women, and children. She specializes in Bob cuts and curly hair. Michelle has curly hair herself so she knows firsthand how to manage and maintain it. She provides many services which include color, foil highlights, balayage, and ombre. She also loves doing special occasion hair such as prom, homecoming or weddings. She will even travel on-site, if needed. Michelle is versed in all types of braids, as well, and offers shampoo and styles. Her goal is to make you feel and look your very best each and every time you visit the salon!

Jenny has been a licensed cosmetologist in WV since 2000. She prides herself on developing and maintaining close relationships with her strong and loyal following. Jenny’s passion is helping you and your family look and feel your best at all times.
